Franciscan University Altar Server's Guild

With the expansion of the university and the chapel, CTK is serving more students than ever.

The Altar Server's Guild at Franciscan University is looking for men who aren't afraid of a higher calling. If you are seeking a way to put your faith into action and contribute your strengths to the most sacred moment on campus, your place is at the altar.

The Supreme Gift

There is no action on earth more significant than the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass. To serve at the altar is to step out of the noise of the world and into the presence of the Divine. You aren't just assisting a priest; you are ministering to The Sacred Mystery. It is a profound gift to be the hands and feet that support the liturgy, ensuring that every movement reflects the glory of the Eucharist.

Strength in Reverence

True reverence isn't passive. It's disciplined. Serving requires a focused mind, a steady hand, and a heart set on excellence. It is a manly tradition of service that demands reliability, brotherhood, and a deep respect for the sacred rituals that have shaped centuries of faith.

When you put on the cassock and surplice, you join a brotherhood of men dedicated to the highest form of service. You learn that leadership often begins with the humility to serve.

Why Join the Guild?

  • Deepen Your Faith: Experience the Mass from the sanctuary, gaining a front-row perspective on the mysteries of our faith.
  • Find Brotherhood: Connect with other Franciscan men who value tradition, discipline, and spiritual growth.
  • Share Your Gifts: Use your time and focus to contribute to the beauty and order of our university's liturgical life.

The altar server has a privileged place in liturgical celebrations. Those who serve at Mass present themselves to a community. They experience from close at hand that Jesus Christ is present and active in every liturgy.

-Pope St. John Paul II
